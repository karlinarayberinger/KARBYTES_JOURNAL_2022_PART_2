INSECURITY. That seems to be the word which sums up what my 2022 journals are mostly about. Other people have said that I was being “too insecure” and to “stop being insecure” and that I am in a messy predicament because of “my insecurity”.

Rather an argue against other people that I am not insecure, I will just admit that I am. Perhaps I am not as insecure as some people think I ought to be given what they think I know (and given all they know). Some people might want me to be more insecure than I currently am and are going out of their way to make me more visibly insecure to others so that I can be ridiculed for being insecure and used as a public example for what not to end up like. Perhaps some people think I deserve to be punished more than I have for whatever crimes I have committed. Perhaps they think that law enforcement has not been punitive enough towards me and such civilians have decided to take matters into their own hands by attempting to make my life more difficult, painful, and unfulfilling for me than it otherwise would be. That alone is enough to call me insecure; the fact that I harbor such “conspiracy theories”.

I have a lot of “conspiracy theories” which I would simply call hypotheses. Having them is entertaining and sometimes even uplifting. I like to use my imagination to concoct science fiction stories to play in the background of my mind while I deal with the urgent drudgery of “real life”.

Because of what I have said, written, or thought and had data mined by mind hacking technologies, people can use whatever information there is about me as they like; to cherry pick the facts which support their agenda. People can also impersonate me with human actors and robots and use that to build a particular reputation of me to attack and to control.

I invite anyone to say whatever they want even if it is an outright lie. I condone radical free speech to the point that there is nothing I would have censored from the public. If some information hurts someone’s virgin mind, oh well. They will just have to deal with being traumatized and/or misinformed. More research and more skepticism is the cure to small mindedness and being fed bullshit information. It is up to each able bodied adult (and child) to do its own research and to back up its learnings and to publish its own content. I hope eventually all the invalids will be saved from being “locked in” asylum patients with brain-to-computer prosthetics which restore mobility, sensory functions, and cognitive ability.

Anyway, I hope that I can just treat whatever I did in the past like its no longer troublesome now. If I said or did upsetting things in the past, I will do my best to pretend I never did nor said such things. I will just hit that magic “reset” button when I feel it is time to end some shitty process I was the effector of. I might “start” as a relatively blank slate who is calm, poised, and ready to take on any experience.
